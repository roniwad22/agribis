What Problems Does This Actually Solve?

Based on research from 74 sources on Uganda's agribusiness challenges

Price Opacity

Only 15% of farmers have pre-harvest market info. USSD price checks change this.

Trust Deficit

No receipts, no records, no accountability. Digital purchase ledger creates a paper trail.

Post-Harvest Losses

40% of produce lost. Batch aggregation and marketplace speed up the sale cycle.

Payment Delays

Farmers wait weeks or months. Escrow guarantees payment on delivery confirmation.

Quality Disputes

No grading standards. Quality grades at point of purchase create shared expectations.

Traceability Gaps

Buyers can't verify origin for export. Batch-to-farmer traceability solves this.
