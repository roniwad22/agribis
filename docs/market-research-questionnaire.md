# Agri-Bridge Market Research Questionnaire

**Purpose:** Validate whether retail market sellers (Nakasero, Kalerwe) have a sourcing problem Agri-Bridge can solve. Do NOT pitch the app — just listen and learn.

**Target:** 10 sellers at Nakasero, 10 at Kalerwe, 5 agents. ~2-3 days of visiting.

**Approach:** Buy something small from their stall first. Chat casually. Don't mention your app.

---

## Questionnaire A: Market Sellers (Nakasero/Kalerwe)

### About Their Business (warm-up)
1. What do you sell? How long have you been here?
2. How much stock do you buy per day? (in kg or basins/sacks)
3. What time do you buy? Where from?

### How They Source (key section)
4. Who do you buy from — one person or several? How did you find them?
5. Do they deliver to you, or do you go pick up?
6. How do you decide what to buy each morning? (price? freshness? what sold yesterday?)
7. Do you ever fail to get enough stock? How often? What do you do when that happens?
8. Do you know the farm-gate price of what you're buying? (Do you care?)

### Money and Trust
9. Do you pay cash upfront, or do they give you credit? How many days?
10. Have you ever been cheated — wrong weight, bad quality hidden underneath, overcharged?
11. How much do you lose to spoilage per week? (in UGX or %)
12. What's your biggest cost after buying stock? (transport? rent? market fees?)

### The Real Question
13. If you could change ONE thing about how you get your stock, what would it be?
14. Have you ever tried buying directly from a village farmer or agent? Why / why not?
15. Do you use Mobile Money? WhatsApp?

---

## Questionnaire B: Agents/Middlemen Who Supply Markets

1. Who do you sell to — market sellers, processors, exporters, or all?
2. How do you find your buyers? Do they come to you, or do you go to Nakasero/Kalerwe?
3. How do market sellers pay you — cash, MoMo, credit?
4. Do market sellers ever refuse your goods? Why?
5. What's harder: finding farmers to buy from, or finding buyers to sell to?
6. Do you ever have stock you can't sell? What happens to it?
7. If someone could connect you to 10 new market sellers who pay on time, would you pay for that? How much?

---

## How to Interpret Results

| Signal | What it means | Action |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Sellers say "finding stock is hard" | Demand for Agri-Bridge marketplace | Build retail features post-pilot |
| Sellers say "supplier cheats me on weight" | Trust problem — escrow/receipts could help | Adapt current features for retail |
| Sellers say "spoilage" or "rent" is main cost | Platform doesn't solve their real pain | Don't build for retail |
| Sellers say "I have a reliable supplier" | No sourcing problem to solve | Focus on bulk buyers only |
| Agents say "finding buyers is hard" | Marketplace is the right feature | Prioritize marketplace growth |
| Agents say "getting paid is hard" | Escrow is the right feature | Current model is correct |

### Kill Signal
If 8/10 sellers say "I have a reliable supplier, I'm fine" — Nakasero is not your market. Focus on bulk buyers.

### Go Signal
If 5+ sellers say "I can't find consistent quality" or "my supplier cheats me on weight" — real opening for retail expansion.
